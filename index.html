Our Story

A mother's eye for
the perfect saree.

Kavitha Rani started VK Sarees & Boutique with one conviction — that every woman who walks through the door should leave feeling like the fabric was woven just for her. Not a showroom. Not a warehouse. A place where someone actually listens to what you want and knows how to find it.

The collection spans the geography of Indian textiles. Kanjeevaram silks from Tamil Nadu. Pochampally ikats from right here in Telangana. Banarasi weaves. Soft cottons for everyday. And when the saree is chosen, everything else follows under one roof — blouse stitching cut to your measurements, maggam work designed to your taste, and honest advice that comes from decades of dressing women for their most important days.

That's really all there is to it. Just beautiful fabric, skilled hands, and a personal touch that the big stores forgot about.

Kavitha Rani
Founder, VK Sarees & Boutique
